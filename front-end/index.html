BMI

what is BMI?

Body Mass Index (BMI) is a widely used measurement that helps determine whether an individual has a healthy body weight relative to their height. It is a simple and quick way to categorize people into different weight groups, which can provide insights into potential health risks associated with being underweight, normal weight, overweight, or obese.

How is BMI calculated?

BMI is calculated using the following formula:

BMI = weight(kg) / height(cm sq)


Alternatively, if you prefer to use pounds and inches:

BMI = weight(lbs) x 703 / height(inches sq)
